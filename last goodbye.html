last goodbye

9/22/23

i've given you the last of your things, and with it all my obligation to you

it feels appropriate that i should see you the morning after i cut all ties


after giving up looking for that broken headlight, of course i would see the broken tailight

the one you got backing into my house the morning after you lied to me, slept with me, and lied to me some more


i think that this is the last time i will write for you

my therapist told me to write something to never send to you, to say some things i wish i had said when we broke up


i will, but i will also leave a lot of it here, in case you ever decide to check


i'm sorry that you didn't feel comfortable telling me anything

i'm sorry that you felt like that was the best option

i'm sorry that you didn't put in more effort until the eleventh hour

i'm sorry that we couldn't make things work


i would hope you have some regrets, but i sincerely doubt i'll ever get to hear them


part of my heart will always be with you

i will never be that close to someone again


if it's meant to be, i'm sure we will see each other again

until then, goodbye


have a nice life
