this is not a romcom kind of love

this is a bastard child of obsession and desperation


the opposite of certainty, hanging onto every word you say and every song you post, hoping it means reconciliation


fighting the urge to beg you to see me, knowing that it wouldn't fix anything


the knowledge deep down that i have irrevocably destroyed the most beautiful love i could ever know


and i'm scared i'll never stop looking for that broken headlight


i don't know any other way to love than desperately
